Property Values & Covid19

In our team we tried to pick topics we all care about. Collectively, we've learned how data analysis tells a story. We use programming languages like Python, Javascript, SQL in our analysis of data. We also use Machine Learning to make predictions.

In our latest project,Proverty Values & Covid19, we examined property values during the time if COVID19. We reviewed Interest Rates, Deaths and Cases to make predictions and analyze the relationships between these variables.

Timeline of Some Covid19 Events:

January 21st 2020

United States confirms its first case in Washington state, a man who traveled to the Wuhan area.

March 13th 2020

President Trump declared a national emergency.

April 2nd 2020

Cases topped a million, and millions lose their jobs. As several states implement mandatory stay at home orders.

July 6th 2020

U.S. deaths surpassed 130,000. Several States begin phased re-openings.

August 27th 2020

The CDC notifies public health officials around the US to prepare to distribute a potential coronavirus vaccine as soon as late October.

October 20th 2020

The United States reports 58,387 new COVID-19 cases in a 24-hour period, its highest figure since July
